A brief history of the school.

The school was founded by Late. Rev. E. Merithung Mozhui in 1972 when there were only few private schools in Wokha district. Starting with kindergarden classes at the beginning, the school gradually reached class X in 1987 and eventually recieved recognition from the Government in 1993.
